## Core Responsibilities

### Research Documentation

TXBD maintains research papers, technical investigations, conceptual studies, and development findings generated throughout the TensoraMax ecosystem.

### Project Archives

Projects may be archived for historical preservation regardless of completion status.

Archived materials may include:

- Source code
- Technical specifications
- Concepts
- Design notes
- Development logs
- Prototypes

### Experimental Records

Experimental systems, prototype technologies, and exploratory developments may be cataloged within TXBD for future reference.

### Historical Preservation

Major milestones, releases, breakthroughs, incidents, and ecosystem evolution are recorded for historical continuity.

## Classification Framework

### Stable

Fully documented and operational systems.

### Experimental

Active research or testing initiatives.

### Restricted

Limited-access systems requiring additional review.

### Unstable

Projects demonstrating unpredictable behavior or incomplete development.

### Archived

Historically preserved projects that are no longer actively maintained.
